Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Ari Global Industries handles information provided through this website and explains our commitment to responsible communication and privacy practices.

Last Updated: June 22, 2026

Introduction

Ari Global Industries respects the privacy of visitors, clients, suppliers, and business partners. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be used when you interact with our website or communicate with us regarding our products and services.

By accessing this website, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described within this Privacy Policy.

Information You Voluntarily Provide

Visitors may choose to contact Ari Global Industries through the communication methods provided on this website, including email, telephone, or other available contact channels.

Information voluntarily shared by visitors may include names, company details, business requirements, contact information, or project-related inquiries necessary for responding to requests and conducting business discussions.

Website Usage Information

This website is primarily informational in nature and does not require users to create accounts, register, or submit personal information in order to access its content.

Like most websites, basic technical information may be processed automatically by web browsers, hosting providers, content delivery services, or internet infrastructure providers for operational and security purposes.

Such information may include browser type, device information, pages visited, referring websites, IP addresses, and general usage statistics. Ari Global Industries does not actively use this information to personally identify visitors.

Business Communications

Information provided during inquiries, supplier discussions, sourcing requests, quotation requests, procurement activities, or project-related communications may be used solely for legitimate business purposes.

This may include responding to inquiries, evaluating requirements, preparing quotations, coordinating sourcing activities, facilitating project discussions, and maintaining ongoing business communication.

Cookies

Ari Global Industries does not intentionally deploy advertising cookies, marketing trackers, behavioral profiling systems, or user-targeting technologies through this website.

However, visitors should be aware that certain technologies used by web browsers, hosting providers, embedded services, content delivery networks, analytics platforms, or third-party integrations may utilize cookies or similar technologies as part of their normal operation.

Visitors may control cookie preferences through their browser settings if desired.

Third-Party Websites

This website may contain links to external websites, business resources, partner websites, supplier information, mapping services, or other third-party platforms.

Ari Global Industries does not control the content, privacy practices, policies, or security measures of external websites. Visitors are encouraged to review the privacy policies of any third-party services they access.

Data Security

Ari Global Industries takes reasonable measures to maintain the security and integrity of business communications and information received through legitimate business interactions.

However, no method of internet transmission, electronic communication, or data storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Accordingly, visitors acknowledge that transmission of information occurs at their own discretion.

Information Sharing

Ari Global Industries does not sell, rent, or trade personal information obtained through this website.

Information may only be shared where reasonably necessary for legitimate business activities, supplier coordination, service execution, legal compliance, or protection of business interests.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for business and commercial purposes and is not directed toward children.

Ari Global Industries does not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website.

Changes To This Privacy Policy

Any updates will be reflected by revising the "Last Updated" date displayed on this page.
